How to use: pick a tool/terrain on the left, then click a cell to paint, or click-drag to paint many. Right-click erases to floor. The Spawn point tool toggles a numbered chef on a floor tile (order = cook 0,1,2…). Fill flood-fills a contiguous same-terrain region with the active terrain. Source / station / terminal tiles render as a counter with their icon on top.
